# Introduction

Welcome to [z3us](https://z3us.com) documentation. Here you will find information on how to use our wallet, interact with it via dapps or create one and integrate it with [z3us](https://z3us.com).

## Why z3us ?

[z3us](https://z3us.com) was created to impvrove user experiance while interacting with [radix](https://www.radixdlt.com/) blockchain. It purpose is to ensure your security while being fully transparent. Our codebase is opensource and everyone can review our work.
